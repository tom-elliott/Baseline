Baseline Build and Renovate are a South East London based company.

Our team are qualified and experienced in complete home renovations, bathroom & shower room refurbishments, room conversions, building work, electricals as well as boilers and various central heating systems.

We serve mainly residential clients and commercial properties within South East, South and East London area. We've been serving these areas for nearly two decades now and have built up a reputation for being efficient, trust worthy, reliable and clean.

We have a quick turnaround time with zero compromise on standards and finish. Everyone at Baseline prides themselves on personal service; from a little repair to renovating an entire house, we treat every job as important, and due to this we've found 75% of our work coming from repeat clients.

We've worked hard to build this trust with our clients by consistently providing second to none service, and always offering exceptional quality workmanship to match. Our reputation is our greatest asset, and we do everything we can to maintain it.
